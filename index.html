Welcome to my Website. I’m Diego Tuzi an enthusiastic guy who loves digital technologies. I am an Engineer with a bachelor and a master degree in Telecommunications. I’m also a licensed professional Italian Engineer (since 2021) and a licensed Italian Journalist (since 2008).

This Website is just a way to practice my writing skills in different languages. I’m not only referring to human languages, such as Italian or English, but I’m also referring to computer languages. For example, what you’re reading right now was written using Markdown. I’ve been studying English, I’m improving day by day but far from perfection. So, if you find any mistake, I’ll be happy to receive your feedback.
